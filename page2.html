ABOUT US

Grace Voice of Truth is a Christian-based non-profit organization that is supported by contributions from friends and partners of the outreach.

  1. What We Do - Grace Voice of Truth is dedicated to sharing God's plan of Salvation, Love, and life-changing message of the Bible to the world through Radio and Various Media Productions.
  2. Our Mission - Grace Voice of Truth exist to share the Gospel and the Love of God that is in Christ Jesus. Through media we teach God's people how to apply biblical truth to every aspect of their lives and encourage them to serve the world around them.
  3. What We Believe - The Spirit and life of Christ's infallible Word, is capable to solving every human problems.
